How to use this site?

Click on one of the game symbols to play in one of these options:
  • Start a new game (and wait for others to join)
    • Since you are a guest, you can't create new games
  • If it's your turn to play, you will see a red badge on the game symbol.
  • Join a game that was already opened by other player.
  • Join an active game, even if it's not your turn. Just wait for your turn.
  • You can even watch the progress of games in which you are not a player.
note: You can join a game in more than one role. This allows multiple players in the room to play with each other on one computer.
